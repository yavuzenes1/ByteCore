ByteCore Dictionary

Coronal Mass Ejection (CME)

A massive eruption of plasma (electrically charged gas) and magnetic field from the Sun's corona (outer atmosphere) into space. When directed at Earth, CMEs can cause geomagnetic storms that affect power grids, satellites, and communication systems.

NASA SWAO

A specialized office at NASA Goddard Space Flight Center. Its mission is to provide real-time space weather analysis and forecasting for NASA missions (including Artemis and future Mars missions) to ensure the safety of astronauts and spacecraft.

Stereo

A NASA solar observation mission that uses two nearly identical spacecraft (STEREO-A and STEREO-B) orbiting the Sun. Its main goal is to capture stereoscopic (3D) images of the Sun to study the structure and evolution of Coronal Mass Ejections (CMEs).

X-Class Flare

The most intense category of solar flares, based on their peak soft X-ray output. Flares are classified by letters (A, B, C, M, X), where each letter represents a tenfold increase in energy output. X-class flares are the most powerful and can cause severe, long-lasting radio blackouts and significant radiation storms near Earth.

CSA

The national space agency of Canada. It is actively involved in space weather monitoring and research, primarily through the development and operation of scientific instruments and ground-based monitoring networks in Canada.
