About the Game

ByteCore is an immersive choice-based story game. Your decisions lead to different endings — you shape your character’s destiny.

How to Play

Each scene contains a question and 4 options. When you hover over an option, the background changes. When you click, you move to the next scene. Every choice affects the story.

Developer’s Note

This game focuses on storytelling and interactive experience. Updates and new chapters will be added over time.
